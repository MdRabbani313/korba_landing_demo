About Us

Serving Korba Since Decades

Korba Municipal Corporation is committed to providing efficient civic services, urban development, and fostering a high quality of life for all residents of the power hub of Chhattisgarh.

From sanitation to infrastructure, citizen services to digital governance—we work round the clock to make Korba a model municipal corporation.
